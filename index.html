How It Works

A comprehensive guide to onboarding your school with Testora.

1

Download & Install

Get started by downloading the Testora installer for your server and client machines. Our lightweight application installs in minutes on any Windows-based system.

  • check_circle Available for x64 and x86 architectures
  • check_circle Single-click deployment
2

Create School Account

Register your institution to centralize all exam data. Set up your school profile, branding, and academic sessions to tailor the experience to your needs.

3

Administrator Setup

Bulk import students and staff members using our intelligent CSV importer. Assign roles, and classes with a few clicks.

4

Session & Exam Configuration

Define your testing environment. Set time limits, number of questions, shuffling rules, and enable security features like the Lockdown Browser.

5

Set Exams & Passcodes

Publish your exams and generate secure passcodes. These unique identifiers ensure that only authorized students can access specific subjects at the scheduled time.

6

Students Take Exams

Students log in, enter their passcode, and begin testing. Administrators can monitor real-time progress and connectivity status from the central dashboard.
